Welcome to First Touch Ministry

We are a community of believers passionate about touching God through worship and touching people through love and service. At First Touch Ministry, we believe that every person matters and that together, we can make a lasting impact in our community and beyond.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church family to call home, we invite you to experience the warmth and love of our community. Come as you are — you belong here.
